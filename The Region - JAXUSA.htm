The Jacksonville Region

Our region is comprised of seven unique and thriving counties that work collaboratively together to attract businesses and labor.

1,100+ Miles of Waterways

Low Cost Tax Climate

Top Ranked Public Schools Districts

The Region’s Waters Inspire Success for Companies

Our region encompasses seven distinctive counties in Northeast Florida – Baker, Clay, Duval, Flagler, Nassau, Putnam and St. Johns.

The Jacksonville region is a place of waterside enjoyment that inspires productivity and innovation. Each county offers a variety of diverse qualities and economic strengths, coupled with a variety of housing and recreation amenities, that create a superior quality of life and a productive and skilled workforce.

Learn more about the distinguishing attributes and assets that each county provides and contributes to our growing region.
